Organization Bylaws

Article I — Name & Purpose

Primetime Softball Organization exists to provide competitive travel fastpitch softball opportunities for youth athletes across its member teams.

Article II — Membership

Membership is open to players and families rostered on a Primetime team in good standing with organization policies and fee obligations.

Article III — Board of Directors

The board consists of the President, Vice President, Treasurer, Secretary, and team representatives as outlined on the Board page.

Article IV — Meetings

The board meets regularly during the season; meeting schedules and minutes are available to members upon request.

Article V — Amendments

These bylaws may be amended by majority board vote at a scheduled meeting.
