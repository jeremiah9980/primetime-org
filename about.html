About Primetime

Primetime Softball Organization is a select/travel fastpitch program built around three core teams — Primetime 10U, Primetime 12U, and Primetime Elite 12U — united by one standard of coaching, culture, and competition.

Mission

To develop competitive, well-rounded softball players through disciplined coaching, meaningful playing time, and a supportive team-first culture, while preparing athletes for the next level of the game.

Values

  • Discipline and accountability on and off the field
  • Player development over shortcuts
  • Transparent, family-first communication
  • Competing at an elite standard every time we take the field
